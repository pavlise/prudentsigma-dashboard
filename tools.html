Tool 01

Position Sizing Calculator

Calculate your correct position size

Enter your account size, the percentage you are willing to risk, your entry price, and your stop loss. The calculator tells you exactly how many units to buy or sell — keeping your risk consistent regardless of volatility.

Consistent position sizing is one of the most powerful risk management tools available. Most investors know their entry — few know their maximum acceptable loss before entering.

Risk / Reward Calculator

Evaluate the quality of a trade

Enter your entry, stop loss, and profit target. The calculator shows you the exact risk/reward ratio — the single most important number to check before entering any position.

What to look for
Minimum acceptable 1.5 : 1

A trader who takes only 3:1 or better setups can be wrong more than half the time and still be profitable. Risk/reward ratio is the foundation of any sustainable approach to markets.
